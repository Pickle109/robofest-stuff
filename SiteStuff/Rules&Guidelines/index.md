# Rules&Guidelines

[Robofest 2026 Robot parade](https://www.robofest.net/index.php/current-competitions/roboparade)


## Key Rules 

### Robot Requirments (2/2)
The rear of the float must have a flat bumper at least 10cm (3.9") tall and 28 cm (11") wide and be 2.54 cm (1") off the ground so that the robot behind is able to sense reliably your robot using its distance sensors 



The rear bumper must remain over the line throughout the parade course ( straight lines and curves )



Robot must have a reliable program to consistently and efficiently follow a black line on a bright surface
